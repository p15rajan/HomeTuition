Common Questions

We've Got Answers

What subjects do you teach?

We specialise exclusively in Mathematics (and Applied Mathematics) for classes 6th to 12th across CBSE, ICSE, and IGCSE boards.

Do you offer online or offline classes?

Both options are available. Offline sessions are conducted in Chennai only. Online classes are open to students across India.

What are the class timings?

Evening slots are available from 3:00 PM to 7:30 PM on weekdays and weekends. Timings are flexible and adjusted to suit each student's school schedule.

How are fees structured?

Fees depend on the grade level and board. Discounts are available for group enrollments and long-term commitments. Please contact us for a personalised fee quote.

Do you provide demo classes?

Yes! We offer a free demo class so students and parents can experience the teaching approach before committing. Book yours today →

How do you track student progress?

Through regular assessments, timed practice tests, and one-on-one feedback sessions. Parents receive periodic updates on their child's performance and areas of improvement.

How many students are in each session?

We keep batch sizes very small to ensure personalised attention. Both one-on-one and small group sessions (up to 3 students) are available.
